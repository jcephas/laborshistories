Vanessa Gonzalez @van_zalez is a Master of Architecture candidate at the Princeton SoA ('24) and is a graduate of Barnard College ('19), holding a Bachelor of Arts in Architecture with a minor in Art History. She currently serves on the Executive Board of PU NOMAS as Secretary. Raised in Brooklyn, NY, Vanessa is a Puerto Rican designer whose academic research has explored how diasporic communities have occupied and reshaped spaces and structures within cities. She is passionate about exploring ways in which engagement practices can be incorporated into projects, in order to create well-informed and effective design solutions. She looks forward to working with others to reframe narratives and promote inclusive architectural futures.
