Spaces of Labor,
Spaces of Life

"SPACES OF LABOR, SPACES OF LIFE" is a partial archive of the expereinces of Mozambican Vertragsarbeiter living and working in the German Democratic Republic between 1979 and 1990.

This project has two components: an archive of images and documents (seen here) and an archive of spaces of life and labor. Click on any image, or view maps of "LABOR" or "LIFE" by clicking on the text in the right or left margins.
